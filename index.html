01 — About

A disciplined approach to economic questions.

Modus Economic Consulting LLC was founded to bridge the gap between complex economic theory and practical legal application. Our leadership holds advanced doctoral degrees in economics and statistical analysis, bringing a deep background in quantitative research, peer-reviewed methodology, and forensic investigation to every engagement.

In complex litigation, data is only as strong as the methodology behind it. Modus Economic Consulting LLC provides advanced economic research, financial valuation, and statistical analysis tailored for legal counsel, corporations, and public entities.

Led by economists with rigorous training and strong backgrounds, we translate intricate data structures into clear, authoritative insights that withstand the scrutiny of deposition and trial. Whether evaluating commercial damages, labor and employment disputes, or forensic accounting structures, we deliver objective, data-driven clarity when the stakes are highest.

Based in Texas and serving clients nationwide, our consultants maintain a reputation for precision, analytical depth, and absolute professional integrity.

Principal

Kan Chen, Ph.D.

Dr. Kan Chen is the Managing Director and Co-founder of Modus Economic Consulting LLC. A Ph.D. economist from Vanderbilt University, he brings over a decade of sophisticated financial analysis, macroeconomic forecasting, and econometric modeling to the litigation landscape. Dr. Chen specializes in quantifying economic damages and constructing scenarios based on rigorous data analytics and transparent, defensible methodologies. Prior to founding Modus, he served as Research Director at IMS Legal Strategies, where he led analytical teams in auditing opposing expert reports and translating complex statistical outcomes into clear courtroom narratives. His extensive experience also includes delivering macroeconomic scenarios for systemically important financial institutions. Dr. Chen's unique combination of high-level academic training, regulatory defense experience, and deep litigation consulting background ensures that counsel receives objective, precise expert analysis when the stakes are highest.

02 — Services

Where the analysis is applied.

  • Personal Injury & Wrongful Death Damages Quantification of lost earnings and earning capacity, and present value of life care plans, incorporating scenarios on worklife expectancy, wage growth, mitigation, fringe benefits, and taxes for plaintiffs and defendants.
  • Present Value & Discounting Analysis Rigorous present-value calculations for future earnings, medical costs, and other damages streams, built to withstand cross-examination.
  • Expert Methodology Review & Rebuttal Review of opposing experts' reports to identify methodological and data flaws, supporting Daubert challenges, deposition, and cross-examination strategy.
  • Statistical Consulting & Data Analytics Deploying advanced statistical modeling to find clarity in massive, unstructured datasets. We provide predictive modeling, and rigorous validation studies that form the foundation for complex legal and business strategies.
